About Us

Care4Campus is a non-profit, voluntary network of counselors committed for the care and welfare of the adolescents. We endeavour to answer adolescent issues pertaining to physical, psychological, and spiritual dimensions. We identify and apply practical solutions through relationship building and teamwork.

Our Aim

Care4Campus exists to give the adolescents a safe and confidential place to work through their problems.
Recognizing adolescence as an extremely vulnerable period, we are here to help the Generation neXt. We want you to survive and overcome the hazards of campus life, in a healthy, spiritual, and productive manner.

Our Focus

More adolescents than ever before need help as they pass through these turbulent years of biological and emotional change. And as this extremely vulnerable time has been overlooked, it is taking a huge toll on the Generation neXt.

We have some good news and some bad news for you. The bad news is that you will recognize your hazardous lifestyle. It is something that’s bothering you now. The good news is that there is hope. You can break these destructive patterns that are subtly taking control of you. And then lead a constructive and joyous life. That is the promise we can give you. Care4Campus offers you an opportunity to talk with someone in a confidential and supportive setting.

Realizing that adolescence is packed with promises and responsibilities, we want to redefine these years as the launching pad for life and map a path for long term fulfilment and eternal impact. Care4Campus challenges you to lay claim to a brighter future, starting today.
