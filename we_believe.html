We Believe

  • Addictions begin as sinful choices (habits), which over time progress into slavery (obsessions), and disease (compulsions & addictions). Unhealthy habits produce a multitude of painful consequences that affect the mind, body, and spirit.
  • Only through Jesus Christ can one experience lasting victory over habits, or obsessions, or compulsions, or addictions.
  • God has supplied his people with a wide array of treatment options to meet the complex needs of those in any level of the problem.
  • God provides the strength to overcome a destructive lifestyle, but it is up to the individual to take the necessary steps to break its patterns.
