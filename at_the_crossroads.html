At The Crossroads

A painful journey:

Life’s a journey. For most part, you’ve been going alone constructing your own road. It was a painful path littered with broken relationships and forgotten dreams, and somewhere along the way your self respect had been trampled. You have unresolved dilemmas and conflicts for which answers have not yet been found.

You are not alone:

Today you’ve come to a place where the road diverges into two. One of these roads you had been travelling alone till today. The other is a road that is new. But you are not alone here. The person who claimed to be God is standing before you. He is also a human like you and me. He is the person who basically said, "If you follow me, I’ll show you life like you’ve never dreamed it could be."

Committed to never leave you:

As for the commitment he makes with you, he will not break. He knows your struggles in life. He completely understands your failures, weaknesses, temptations and strengths. He loves you with a love that is not because of anything in you. His very nature and essence is love and His love toward you is uncaused and unconditional. He seeks your highest good. He is totally committed to you – committed every moment of every day. And best of all he is committed to never betray you and never desert you, all your life.
